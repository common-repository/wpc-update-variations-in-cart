WPC Update Variations In Cart gives your customer power to change variation in cart.

== Description ==

**WPC Update Variations In Cart** plugin effectively allows your customers to easily change the chosen variations in the cart page to the more preferable ones without the need to go back to the product page or remove & re-add products to the cart. It’s super easy to use with no special settings required.

Bringing this small change to your online store can result in huge benefits. First and foremost, it reduces the cart abandonment rate - the worst nightmare of online businesses. If not converted into purchases, all efforts to bring about a great user experience and appealing offers can be useless. You don’t have to worry about cart abandoning or impatient leave of customers when they change their minds. Giving your customers the right to change their choice with ease at the very last step before they make the payment would highly encourage faster checkout and thus, boost your sales.

== Installation ==

1. Please make sure that you installed WooCommerce
2. Go to plugins in your dashboard and select "Add New"
3. Search for "WPC Update Variations In Cart", Install & Activate it
4. Done!
